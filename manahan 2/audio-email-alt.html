In this edition of US Outlook Macrocast, Michael Gapen (Head of US Economics Research) discusses his views on the April employment report and why it points to an economy in expansion, not an economy stalling amid rising recession risks. He also investigates the Fed's assertion that underperformance of core inflation this year can be explained by transitory factors.

Audio is disabled in chrome by default for most websites unless you have a history of playback. You can decide to disable entirely the autoplay policy by setting the Chrome flag "Autoplay Policy" to "No user gesture is required" at chrome://flags/#autoplay-policy. This allows you to test your website as if the user were strongly engaged with your site and playback autoplay would be always allowed.
